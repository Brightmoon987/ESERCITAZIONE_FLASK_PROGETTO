Barony of Loth

The Barony of Loth was a subordinate land to Arleon's,needed to pay taxes and provide it's population for the dominating land(at the time) to make war. Came a time when the barony started to have big problems: Their crops were flooding because of hazardous weather and their land was pillaged by bandits. In all this mess they were threatened by baryans and needed to deliver taxes to Arleon.A helping hand came by the Unseen Society,that soon started in the name of Empress Aurelia revive the dead for various uses.This faction has undead and living beings in it's ranks.
