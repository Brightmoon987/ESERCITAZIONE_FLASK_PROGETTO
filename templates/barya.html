Republic of Barya

Barya is a relatively modern Republic by it's world standards.They have an unique life ethic,completely bound by contracts that define why the Baryans must fight in a situation or do something at all.They even can fight eachoter if they are serving in a contract to another faction. Having developed marvelous technology,the Baryans deploy battalions of musketeers with firearms and soldiers with spearguards,wanting to keep the enemy away to mitigate incoming losses.They have an alliance with the harima,a race that has in itself frighteningly efficient melee specialists to cover the human defects in close distance encounters.The peak research that this faction had employs in the battlefield steel suits with flamethrowers and fearsome mortars.
